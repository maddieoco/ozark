BUFFALO RIVER LEVELS

NPS floating recommendations for Boxley:
Very Low: Less than 3.25 ft.
Moderate: 3.81-5.71 ft.
High 5.71-8.89 ft.
Flood stage: Greater than 8.89 ft
The Boxley gage is best for float trips on the Hailstone and Boxley to Ponca.

NPS floating recommendations for Ponca:
Very Low: Less than 2.4 ft.
Low: 2.40-2.82 ft.
Moderate: 2.82-3.65 ft.
High 3.65-6.00 ft.
Flood stage: Greater than 6 ft. gage height
The Ponca gage is best for float trips in the upper Buffalo: Ponca to Kyles, Steel Creek, and Erbie.

NPS floating recommendations for Pruitt:
Very Low: Less than 4.24 ft.
Low: 4.24-4.69 ft.
Moderate: 4.69-6.59 ft.
High 6.59-8.28 ft.
Flood stage: Greater than 8.28 ft.
The Pruitt gage is best for float trips in the middle Buffalo from Pruitt to the Hwy 65 bridge.

NPS Recommendations for this gage were not available. The recommendations for the Pruitt gage cover this stretch of the river.
